About Us

South Street Under has been part of the Market Station family for over a decade.
What began as a place to bake fresh breads and pastries for the
Tuscarora Mill Restaurant has evolved into Old Town Leesburg's favorite gathering place,
serving breakfast, lunch, coffee, and pastries seven days a week.
